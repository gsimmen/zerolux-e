1. Inform yourself

The abuse of street lighting, with totally inadequate equipment, is the main cause of light pollution. Light pollution is a disaster for astronomy, of course, but also for the balance of life on earth in general. It disrupts the lives of thousands of species, it disrupts insects and the entire food chain.

2.a Geoluxmeter

Geoluxmeter is a geolocated luxmeter. Everyone can make the device and then record a sampling of geolocated light points. just pair it with your smartphone then to clip it on the handle of your bag or your cap.

2.b Spam-box

They send one email per night with the lux level plus a notice explaining how you can reduce light pollution you can attach them under streetlights and like the geoluxmeter they are also geolocated, the gps position is translated into zipcode and then make a request for the municipality's email via ch.ch
